Example of an Off-Center Crater


Sometimes the image will have a crater that is the correct size, but in the wrong place. The image below is an example of an off-center crater.



There are three simple steps for re-centering a crater:
1. Click the "Re-Center" button.
2. Click in the center of the crater
3. If the crater is now centered, click "Confirm", otherwise click "Re-center" to try again or "Cancel" to return to the main screen.
